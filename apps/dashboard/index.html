Features

Everything your bot needs
to feel alive

Not just a chatbot. A full personality engine with memory, voice, and creativity.

💬

Multi-Platform

One bot, everywhere. WhatsApp via Baileys, Telegram Bot API, website widgets, and REST endpoints. All from one config.

🧠

AI Brain

Multi-provider AI routing with automatic fallback. OpenAI, Google, OpenRouter — the best model for each request, at the lowest cost.

📖

Smart Memory

Per-user, per-group memory that remembers what matters. Context compression keeps responses fast and cheap. No raw conversation dumps.

🎙️

Voice Recognition

Auto-transcribe voice notes from WhatsApp and Telegram. Pass the text to your AI engine. Supports Whisper, Google STT, AssemblyAI.

🎨

Image Generation

Generate images directly in chat. Free mode via Pollinations, or premium models from OpenAI and Google. Daily quotas to control costs.

⚡

Smart Reactions

Emoji reactions on low-value messages. Save tokens by reacting instead of replying to filler content. Feels natural, never spammy.
